Welcome to MakeItSo, the walking guide for the Medieval centre of Southamption, UK

There are 3 buttons on the screen:

Bottom Left: Switch on routes

Top Left: Switch on Points of Interests (including the walking route directions)

Top Right: Move the map to a point of interest

The blue bubbles on the map contain information about the sights as you walk the route. The walking route directions are in blue at the top just under the image
